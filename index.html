The artist behind it all

A lifetime devoted to creating.

For over fifteen years, Delara Bitar has been turning blank surfaces into living stories. What began in 2009 as a single art studio in Amioun has grown into two distinct creative worlds — one bursting with color and joy, the other rich with depth and meaning.

Whether she's guiding a child through their first brushstroke, painting Byzantine icons for sacred spaces, or carefully crafting a tattoo that will live on someone's skin forever, Delara approaches each piece with the same dedication: art is never just decoration — it's identity made visible.

— Delara
