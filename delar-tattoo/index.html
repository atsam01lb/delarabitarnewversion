The studio

A space where art meets identity.

Delar Tattoo is the more intimate, more permanent chapter of Delara Bitar's artistic world. Born from a lifelong fascination with line, form, and the human canvas, the studio brings the same dedication you'd find in fine art — to the deeply personal craft of tattooing, henna, and piercing.

Every consultation begins with listening. Every design begins with you. Every needle, every piece of jewelry, every drop of ink is held to clinical standards of safety. This isn't just a tattoo shop — it's where stories become permanent.
