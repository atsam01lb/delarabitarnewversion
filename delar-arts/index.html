Our story

More than a studio — a creative home.

Del' Arts opened its doors in Amioun in 2009 with a simple mission: to make art accessible, joyful, and personal for everyone — from a curious three-year-old to a church needing a hand-painted icon.

Over the years, our walls have witnessed birthday parties bursting with laughter, private classes that turned hobbyists into artists, and quiet hours of focused work on commissions both whimsical and sacred. What stays constant: a deep love for craft, color, and the people who walk through our door.
